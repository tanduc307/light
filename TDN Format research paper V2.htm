Title

The Title Says It All (Annesley, 2010a)

 Be Concise

 Be Clear

 Be Informative

 Use Key Words and Terms Wisely

 Know the Journal and the Target Audience

 Avoid Abbreviations

 

Abstract

Step by step (Sullivan, 2021)

 Sentences 12. Set the stage

 Sentences 3. State the mystery

 Sentence 48. Describe your detective work

 Sentence 9. Solve the mystery

 Sentence 10. Open new vision

 

 

Advice from Thomas M. Annesley (2010b)

1. It should present some information from every section of the body of the research paper

2. It should typically not contain citations or references

3. Tell a story by answering questions

4. Use the correct style and format

5. Create an abstract that stands on its own

6. Write the abstract after completing the main text

7. Avoid abbreviations

 

For more adivce on writing abstract, please read here (Matthews & Matthews, 2008)

 

Introduction

Step by step

1. Broad background and context

2. Review of relevant literature

3. Introduction to the gaps or unknowns you have addressed

4. Explanation of why these gaps are important

5. Your exact research questions or hypotheses

6. Approach, plan of attack, proposed solution

Khi muốn trình bày về giả thuyết nghiên cứu:

 

 We hypothesized that

 We tested the hypothesis that

 We asked whether

 To answer this question,

 This prompted us to investigate whether

 Điều này dẫn chúng tôi đến việc xem xét

 To resolve this apparent difference

 We solved this problem by

 The purpose of our study was

 Mục đích của nghiên cứu này là

 

 Keep the paper clear and succinct (Friedman, 2005)

 Read From research to manuscript: A guide to scientific writing (Katz, 2009)

Khi chuyển ý từ việc đưa ra giả thuyết nghiên cứu đến chuyện làm sáng tỏ những vấn đề còn chữ rõ (gaps) trong chủ đề này thì sử dụng các mẫu câu sau:

 These prior studies show that

 Những nghiên cứu trước cho thấy

 Supporting the theory that

 These studies are important because

 Interestingly,

 More importantly,

 Using this information,

 Yet,

 Unlike

 Whereas it has been shown that

 On the other hand,

 It is unclear

 The question remains, however,

 Although prior studies demonstrated

Method

Step by step

1. Details of any equipment, organisms, reagents, etc. used

2. Names of the manufacturers from which equipment or materials were obtained

3. Details of approval of human or animal research by an appropriate committee

4. Types of statistical and/or computational procedures used

5. Tables with information on experimental or observational conditions

 

Chú đến cách viết làm sao cho người đọc có thể tự lặp lại thí nghiệm (Fidler & Wilcox, 2021)
